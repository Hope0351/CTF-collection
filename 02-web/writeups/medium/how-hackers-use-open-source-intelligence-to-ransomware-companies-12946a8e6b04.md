## Google Hacking


Google Dorking or Google hacking is where you use queries crafted to find specific information that will never appear in the standard searches that we all make every single day. As you can see, adding in search operators such as inurl and filetype along with sensitive keywords such as password, secret, or confidential, can return all sorts of interesting results.
