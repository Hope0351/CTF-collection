## Why Companies Use SIEM?

But before we delve further into Security Information and Event Management (SIEM), let’s discuss what it’s all about.

SIEM, which stands for Security Information and Event Management, is a system designed to collect and correlate logs from various sources. It provides real-time monitoring and event management capabilities, enabling faster threat hunting and incident investigation compared to manual inspection of disparate sources.
