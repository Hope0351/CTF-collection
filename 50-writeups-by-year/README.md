## Why a Temporal Index?

The CTF ecosystem evolves rapidly — challenges that were cutting-edge in 2017 are now beginner-tier. The temporal index lets you:

- **Trace the evolution of a category.** Pwn challenges from 2015 are mostly simple buffer overflows; pwn challenges from 2024 are mostly heap or kernel.
- **Find vintage challenges for foundational practice.** If you're learning pwn, the 2017–2019 picoCTF archives are the easiest entry point.
- **Study recent trends.** The 2024–2026 pages reflect the current state of the art.
