# 🎉 My OSCP+ Journey: From Hack The Box to Certified Ethical Hacker!

## Hey everyone! I’m super excited to finally share some big news — I passed the OSCP+ exam!

This journey wasn’t easy, but it was totally worth it. If you’re someone preparing for OSCP+, or thinking about it, I hope this post gives you some insight and motivation. Let me walk you through how I got started, what helped me most, and how it all ended in success!

### Getting Started: Hack The Box Days

Like many beginners, I started my hacking journey on Hack The Box (HTB). It’s a platform full of virtual machines you can hack legally to improve your skills. When I first started, I had no idea what I was doing — I just kept trying, learning, Googling, and slowly getting better.

Every time I “rooted” a machine or captured a flag, it gave me a huge confidence boost. HTB taught me the basics of enumeration, exploitation, and post-exploitation. I also learned how important it is to take notes, stay organized, and not give up when stuck!
