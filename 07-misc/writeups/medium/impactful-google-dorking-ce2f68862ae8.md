### Overview

If you are beginner in Bug Bounty Hunting, this is section is for you otherwise you can skip this right now.
So, Google Dorking referred as an advanced techineque of finding publicly available data of your target over the internet. Sometimes this data become highly valuable and can be considered as the Information Disclosure Bug on your target.

Google Dorking is finding specifically about your target site and helpful for accessing data such as exposed files such as pdfs, configuration files, login pages or even sql errors of your target if they are exposed over the internet.

Now you’ve basic understanding of Google Dorking and we can start practically searching for your target over the internet.
