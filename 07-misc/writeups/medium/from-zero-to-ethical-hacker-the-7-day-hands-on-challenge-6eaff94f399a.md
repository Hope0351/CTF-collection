# From Zero to Ethical Hacker: The 7-Day Hands-On Challenge

>

Go from curious to capable in one week with these hands-on daily exercises that teach real-world penetration testing skills.

*From Zero to Ethical Hacker: The 7-Day Hands-On Challenge*Picture this: You’re in a job interview for a cybersecurity role. The interviewer asks, *“Can you actually find vulnerabilities, or do you just know the theory?”* Your heart sinks because despite reading countless articles and watching YouTube tutorials, you’ve never actually found a real vulnerability.

Sound familiar?

Hey there, Ivan here from Bucharest. Last month, I witness a developer who knew every OWASP vulnerability by name but froze when I asked him to demonstrate SQL injection on a test application. He’s not alone, most of us consume cybersecurity content passively but never get our hands dirty with the actual work.

That’s exactly why I created this 7-day challenge. No more theory. No more passive learning. Just pure, hands-on ethical hacking that transforms you from curious observer to capable practitioner.
