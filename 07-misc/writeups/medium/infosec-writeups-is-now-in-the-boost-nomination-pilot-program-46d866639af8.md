## What Kind of Stories IW Nominates For Boost


The criteria for Boosting stories is based on certain factors:

### Writer’s experience


Authors should make it a point to establish their credibility and experience in the article. Readers should get a real understanding why YOU are the right person to write about this.

### Value and impact


Add value to the reader’s lives. People should leave your article having learned something cool. Make the titles COMPELLING. Any person who’s casually scrolling through must feel compelled to read your article just by looking at the title.

### Respect for the reader


Respect the reader’s time. Don’t add fillers, emojis, images, or GIFs just for the sake of it. Don’t use clickbait. Write in a compassionate tone, so your posts help the readers.

### Writing and craftsmanship


Structure your stories well. Use [Medium’s in-built editing tools](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DNHF6yTN-3o) to make your story visually appealing and easy to digest.
