It’s often compared to the OSCP, but unlike OSCP, it allows the use of `AI`, `sqlmap`, and focusing heavily on real-world reporting. Here is how I navigated the 10-day exam window and passed.

## The Prerequisites

Unlike some certifications where you can jump straight into the exam, HTB requires you to earn your stripes first.

- The CPTS Path: You must complete the entire job role path on HTB Academy. Depending on your pace, this takes about a month to 40 days of dedicated study.

- Eligibility: Only after completing the path do you unlock the exam attempt.
