## Introduction

The [Decompetition capture the flag](https://decompetition.io/) (2020 ed.) calls on reverse engineers from across the planet to reverse engineer native binaries written in *C*, *C++*, *Go*, *Rust* and *Swift*. The goal is for the contestant to reconstruct the compiled binary into its original source code as best as they can, and the CTF lasted for twenty-four (24) hours.

*Source: [https://archive.is/mCFai](https://archive.is/mCFai)*

I signed up as the user EntropyThot and started my own “team” called the Rocking Philosophers — which basically just consisted of myself 😋

I scored 52nd place (out of 277 entrants). Assuming that my maths are correct, that puts me in the 77th percentile!
