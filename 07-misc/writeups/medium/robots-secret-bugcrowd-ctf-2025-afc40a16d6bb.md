## What is CTF ?


If you’re new hacking or bug hunting, you maybe wondering what is this thing CTF, basically it stands for Capture the Flag(CTF). It just a Hacking competing where you have provided targeted machines of differenent categories like Web, Cryptography, OSINT and other types. You have to hack these machines and then you found a secret text called flag which can be in a specified format like FLAG{I_am_a_customized_flag}.
