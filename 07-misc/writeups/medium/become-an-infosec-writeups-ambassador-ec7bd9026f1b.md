With this program, we aim to have a global team of infosec enthusiasts eager to play their role in helping the infosec community grow. Infosec Writeups will help you make meaningful contributions to the community while encouraging and guiding you on any passion projects you’re pursuing.

### Requirements to apply

- Passion for the Infosec community.

- Active on Twitter within the past year.

- Commitment to reading and learning from new resources.

- Commitment to sharing the best resources and helping the Infosec community grow.

- Having the ability to write content in good English is a plus.

- Having the ability to create graphics and illustrations to share in social media is a plus.

### IW Ambassador Perks

- Exclusive swag.

- Premium subscriptions to Infosec tools and learning programs.

- Gain experience to build, lead, and nurture niche communities.

- Expand your Infosec network and meet lifelong friends.
