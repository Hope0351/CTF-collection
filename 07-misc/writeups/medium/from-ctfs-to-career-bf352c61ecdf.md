The Step-by-Step Guide That Helped Me Learn Cybersecurity

## Who am i ?(a.k.a. Why You Should Even Listen to Me)


I’m a college dropout.


Not the “I partied too hard” type. I left because the system felt broken, slow, and not made for hackers like us.


But I didn’t just drop out and vibe on Netflix — I got to work. CS50 Cybersecurity from Harvard? Done. Bug bounties? Claimed. CTFs? Conquered. Career? Now I’m in one.


This is *exactly* how I did it — step by step, raw and real.

## Step 1: Learn the Basics, But Do It Differently


Forget cramming theory. I started with CS50x: Introduction to Computer Science and then did CS50: Cybersecurity. Both are free and better than 90% of paid bootcamps.


Why? Because they teach you how to *think*, not just what to memorize.


Also, the teaching is so good, you’ll start dreaming in Python.
