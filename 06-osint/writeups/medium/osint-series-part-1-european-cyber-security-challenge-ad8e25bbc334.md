# OSINT Series Part 1 — European Cyber Security Challenge

## While I was scavenging through the internet I came across with The European Cyber Security Challenge (ECSC) and decided to solve some of the challenges in the OSINT.

In this article I am going to provide you with the information on European Cyber Security Challenge, and solve some of the past OSINT related challenges. We will solve two challenges in this writeup:

ECSC 2018 — Image Intelligence — Easy Difficulty
Tools and Techniques: Reverse Search, Google Maps, EXIF Tool, Online Information

ECSC 2018 — Image Intelligence 2 — Medium Difficulty
Tools and Techniques: Reverse Search, Google Maps, Google Earth, EXIF Tool, Online Information

This challenges aims to train on the OSINT tools and techniques as well as having fun to discover hidden things.

## What is European Cyber Security Challenge

The European Cyber Security Challenge is an initiative by the European Union Agency for Cybersecurity (ENISA) and aims at enhancing cybersecurity talent across Europe and connecting high potentials with industry leading organizations.
