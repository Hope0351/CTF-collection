> Part of **CTF-collection** — see [`30-archetypes/README.md`](README.md) for the full archetype index.

## Overview

Curated directories of CTF resources — frameworks, tools, writeups, practice platforms, and learning materials. They are the natural starting point for entering the ecosystem.

Awesome-lists are the discovery layer of the ecosystem. `apsdehal/awesome-ctf` is the canonical entry point and tends to be the highest-starred repository in the CTF category on GitHub.

## How to Use These Repositories

Awesome-lists are best used as **discovery tools**. When you're not sure where to start in a new category or sub-topic, consult the relevant awesome-list — they are explicitly designed for browsing.

`apsdehal/awesome-ctf` is the canonical entry point and should be the first bookmark for anyone entering the CTF ecosystem. The other awesome-lists cover specific niches (mobile, hardware, ICS, blockchain) and are useful when you're going deep on a specific category.
