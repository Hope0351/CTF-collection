So, at this point, all I had to do was enter those quirky symbols into the input box on the dcode.fr website, and voila! It worked like a charm, giving me this output: FOURSIX . SEVENSIXEIGHT , -NINETWO . ONETWOFOUR


After converting these words to numbers, I got the final output: 46.768 , -92.124, which is nothing other than GPS coordinates! 🌍📍 Where 46.768 represents the Latitude, and -92.124 represents the Longitude. Simple and ingenious! 🌐😄


The final step was to input those coordinates, 46.768 for latitude and -92.124 for longitude, into any GPS coordinate converter. And there you have it! 🌍


The result came out in DMS (degrees, minutes, seconds) format, which reads: “N 46° 46' 4.8'’ W 92° 7' 26.399'’.” 🌐
